Brent McAllister Design Inc.

With a Bachelor of Interior Design Degree from the University of Manitoba and a background in Engineering and Business Administration I built a strong educational foundation towards my successful career as an Interior Designer.

After several years of working with Government Contracts and leading a design team in a large design firm in Ottawa I branched out on my own to begin Brent McAllister Design Inc. in 1989 where my focus has been on both residential and commercial design.

Services

Design is a series of compromises.
Additions & Construction

From build out or build up of current structures to full building design solutions enables the client to grow into their current space or see an imagined space come to life.

Sustainable Solutions

A design approach to include the materials already available. It's the responsibility of a Designer to be conscious of and incorporate sustainable solutions forward in each project to minimize the impact on the environment, the economy and on society.

Residential Design

Client focussed consultation, strategic selection of finishes, custom interior renovations to include kitchen, bathroom and basement, and full service project management.

Commercial Design

Office planning solutions, retail spaces, restaurants, daycare centres and healthcare facilities to include medical clinics, dental offices, optometry clinics, hearing centres and physiotherapy centres.
